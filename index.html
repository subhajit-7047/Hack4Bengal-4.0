Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my mental health data on CogniCare?

Your data is protected by enterprise-grade encryption and complies with HIPAA standards. We never share your personal information without your explicit consent.

What types of mental health assessments are available?

We offer a wide range of AI-powered assessments including depression, anxiety, stress, bipolar disorder, ADHD, and more. Each test is clinically validated and regularly updated.

How do I connect with a mental health professional?

You can book online consultations through our platform. Our AI matching system will connect you with the most suitable therapist based on your needs and preferences.

Are the online therapy sessions confidential?

Yes, all therapy sessions are completely confidential and conducted through our secure, encrypted platform. Your privacy is our top priority.

How often should I track my mental health?

We recommend daily check-ins for optimal monitoring. Our AI system analyzes patterns and provides personalized insights based on your regular updates.

What's included in the premium membership?

Premium members get unlimited access to all assessments, priority therapy bookings, advanced AI insights, personalized wellness plans, and exclusive content.

Can I access CogniCare on mobile devices?

Yes, CogniCare is fully responsive and works seamlessly on all devices. You can also download our mobile app for iOS and Android.

What should I do in a mental health emergency?

For immediate help, use our 24/7 crisis support feature or contact emergency services. We provide emergency contact information and direct connection to crisis counselors.
